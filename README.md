# Sustainability Governance Atlas

## Mapping Transition Tensions and Implementation Conditions Across Europe

### Overview

The Sustainability Governance Atlas is a research platform designed to identify structural pressures, governance challenges, and implementation conditions shaping sustainability transformations across European countries.

The project integrates environmental, economic, social, and governance indicators to explore where sustainability transitions may face heightened implementation challenges and competing policy priorities.

---

## Research Question

How do structural socioeconomic conditions influence the implementation of sustainability transitions across Europe?

---

## Core Concepts

### Transition Tension Index (TTI)

Measures broader socioeconomic pressures surrounding sustainability transitions.

### Transition Friction Index (TFI)

Measures implementation conditions that may complicate policy delivery and uptake.

### Friction Typologies

Comparative profiles identifying dominant implementation pressures across countries.
